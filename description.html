An Interactive Tool to Visualize 5+ Years (2019 - 2024) of Historical Hourly Weather Data

- Select which year and month to query a subset of the data.
- Determine the US weather station to display, as well as what weather metric you would like to graph on the y-axis.
- Inspired by the Bokeh Interactive Apps on GitHub.
- Information is sourced from NOAA Data Records periodically.
